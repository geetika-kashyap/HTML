Sundar Pichai

Pichai Sundararajan (born June 10, 1972), better known as Sundar Pichai, is an Indian-born American business executive. He is the chief executive officer (CEO) of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google.

  • Pichai joined Google in 2004 as a product manager and has navigated his way through the company over the years.

  • Before his promotion, Pichai headed the products and engineering department at Google.

  • Sundar Pichai has been chosen as he is one of the most remarkable current business leaders (Vijayakumar & Cunningham, 2016).

  • His transformational leadership continues to promote growth, better performance, and steer innovation at Google.

*Biography*

  • Pichai was born in India at Chennai, Madurai on 12th July 1972 (Goldman, 2015).

  • His father worked as an electrical engineer.

  • His mother worked as a stenographer.

  • Pichai attended his early and high school education at Chennai.

  • He later pursued higher education at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania, and at Stanford University respectively.
